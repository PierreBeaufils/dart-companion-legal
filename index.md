# Privacy Policy - DartRise

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DartRise ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the mobile application.

## 1. Data We Collect

### A. Data you create in the App
- Game and training data (scores, match history, statistics, progress).
- Tournament data you enter (for example: tournament name, players, brackets, results).

Most gameplay data is stored locally on your device.

### B. Data used for shared tournaments
When you use tournament sharing features, the App uses Supabase to store and retrieve shared tournament data.
- Shared tournament content can include tournament metadata and results you choose to publish.
- The App may use an anonymous authentication identifier to let the creator manage shared tournaments.

### C. Support contact data
If you contact support by email, we receive the information you provide in your message (for example, your email address and message content).

## 2. Data We Do Not Intentionally Collect

- We do not require account creation for normal app usage.
- We do not intentionally collect precise location data.
- We do not intentionally collect contacts, photos, or microphone recordings.
- We do not currently serve third-party advertising in the App.

## 3. How We Use Data

We use information to:
- Provide core app features (scoring, training, tournaments).
- Save and sync shared tournament content when you use sharing features.
- Maintain app reliability and respond to support requests.

## 4. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Where applicable, we process data based on:
- Performance of a contract (providing app functionality you request).
- Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, product maintenance).
- Consent, when required by law.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited data with service providers strictly to operate the App, such as:
- **Supabase** (backend/database for shared tournament features).

These providers process data under their own terms and security controls.

## 6. Data Retention

- Local app data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- Shared tournament data is retained while needed to provide sharing features, or until deleted by us or by feature controls.
- Support emails may be retained as needed to handle your request and legal obligations.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the minimum age required by local law without parental consent. If you believe a child provided personal data unlawfully, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain personal data.

To make a request, contact us at: **helcoralabs@gmail.com**

## 10. International Transfers

If data is processed outside your country, we apply appropriate safeguards as required by applicable law.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new "Last updated" date.
